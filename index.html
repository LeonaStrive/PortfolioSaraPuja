Klinik Mora

Company Profile Design

The Mora Clinic UI/UX design was created to make it easier for prospective patients to access important information about the clinic's services, such as operating hours, doctor schedules, types of services, and contact details for reservations. This design was specifically created for a responsive website platform that adapts to various devices. The target users are members of the general public who need quick and reliable access to healthcare services. The design process was carried out using Figma, with a completion time of three weeks. The final outcome includes high-fidelity designs accompanied by interactive prototypes.

Mora Traveling

Website Travel Apps Design

Mora Traveling is a UI/UX case study designed for a web-based airline and train ticket booking platform. The goal of this project is to help users easily explore travel schedules, select departure times and seats, and complete payments through a clean and intuitive interface. This design focuses on improving the overall booking experience by simplifying user interactions. The project was created using Figma, with Lottie animations added to incorporate smooth yet effective motion elements that guide user attention and enhance engagement. The project was designed for desktop use and completed in approximately two months. The final outcome is a high resolution interactive prototype.

Delivery Food Apps

Mobile Apps Design

The Food Ordering App is a UI/UX design case study designed to simulate the experience of ordering food through a mobile app. The app allows users to explore various food and beverage options, place orders, and easily specify delivery locations. Designed using Figma, this project also utilizes Lottie animations to enhance mobile interactions with dynamic and lightweight animations, thereby improving user satisfaction and functional clarity. The app was developed for mobile platforms, with a focus on usability and visual appeal. The design process took about two months, resulting in a high-resolution mobile prototype.
